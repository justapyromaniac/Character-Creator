Character creator

This is the new and improved interface of the character creator, right now it doesn't do a thing.
It's just pretty but it does have more info.

Class

Pick the class and customize it to fit your preferences.
Also pay attention to any info displayed such as the level table and basic info.

Race

Here you pick the general looks of your character in the ways of height, weight, age and the aesthetic of your race.
Also you'll choose several race specific things to customize it fully. Make sure to read the race info already displayed.

Attributes

These decide your characters ability modifiers during skill checks, attacks as well as your HP.
Make sure to check what the reccomended attributes for your class are.
