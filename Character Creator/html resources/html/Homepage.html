Class

Your character's class, pick the class and choose a lot of class specific data along the gear. Also pay attention to any info displayed such as the level table and basic info

Race

Your character's race, here you pick the general looks of your character in the ways of height, weight, age and the aesthetic of your race. Also you'll choose several race specific things to customize it fully. Make sure to read the race info already displayed

Attributes

The attributes of your character, these decide your characters ability modifiers during skill checks, attacks as well as your HP. Make sure to check what the reccomended attributes for your class are
